Our Approach

We specialize in land contracts and short/medium/long‑term rentals. Our priority is predictable, transparent terms for both sides.

  1. 1) Lead & Fit

    Tell us what you need (sell a parcel, rent, or explore installments). We do a quick fit check and pull county records.

  2. 2) Clear Numbers

    You get a simple term sheet (price, timeline, fees). No surprises: survey & deed costs are handled up‑front.

  3. 3) Paperwork

    We draft clean documents in plain English (with the precise legal language where it matters). You sign in person or e‑sign.

  4. 4) Close & Move‑In

    We coordinate recording, keys, or lot access. You get receipts and a tidy folder of PDFs.

Looking at a specific parcel?
